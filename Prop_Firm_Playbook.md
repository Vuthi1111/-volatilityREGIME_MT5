# Prop Firm Trading Playbook
## 1. Strategy Overview
**Style:** Discretionary Price Action combined with Order Flow / Volume Profile.
**Asset Classes:** Indices (e.g., NQ, ES, US30) and Commodities (e.g., Gold, Oil).
**Timeframes:**
- **Higher Timeframe (HTF) - 1H/4H:** Trend direction, major liquidity pools, and Volume Profile Value Areas.
- **Medium Timeframe - 15m:** Intraday market structure, VWAP trends, and setup formation.
- **Execution Timeframe - 5m/1m:** Entry triggers, volume confirmation, and tight risk management.

---

## 2. Risk Management (Prop Firm Specific)
Prop firms typically impose a **5% Daily Drawdown** and **10% Max Drawdown**. Survival is the primary objective.

- **Risk Per Trade:** 0.25% - 0.50% of the initial account balance.
- **Max Trades Per Day:** 3 (If 2 consecutive losses occur, stop trading for the day).
- **Daily Loss Limit:** 1.5% - 2.0% (Well below the prop firm's 5% limit to prevent account breaches).
- **Minimum Reward-to-Risk (R:R):** 2:1. (E.g., risking $50 to make $100).

---

## 3. The A+ Setups (The "Playbook")

### Setup 1: The Liquidity Sweep & Volume Rejection
**Context:** The market is approaching a major HTF high/low (Support/Resistance).
1. **The Sweep:** Price breaks the high/low to trap retail breakout traders and trigger stop losses.
2. **Volume Confirmation:** Look for a high volume node (climax volume) on the sweep, followed by an immediate aggressive rejection back into the range.
3. **Entry:** Enter on the pullback (e.g., to the 15m or 5m Fair Value Gap / Order Block or VWAP).
4. **Stop Loss:** Just above/below the sweep wick.
5. **Take Profit:** Opposite side of the range or next major liquidity pool (targeting at least 2R).

### Setup 2: Value Area Rejection / VWAP Bounce (Trend Continuation)
**Context:** The market is in a clear trend on the 15m chart.
1. **The Setup:** Price pulls back to the Volume Profile Point of Control (POC) or the daily VWAP.
2. **Volume Confirmation:** Selling/Buying volume dries up as it approaches the level (low volume pullback), and delta shifts in favor of the trend.
3. **Entry:** Enter when price action confirms the bounce (e.g., engulfing candle or pin bar on 5m).
4. **Stop Loss:** Below the POC / VWAP and swing low.
5. **Take Profit:** Recent swing high/low (targeting at least 2R).

## 4. Trading Rules & Routine

### Pre-Market Routine (30 mins before open)
- Check economic calendar (ForexFactory) for high-impact news (CPI, NFP, FOMC). **DO NOT trade 15 mins before/after major news.**
- Mark HTF Support/Resistance, previous day high/low, and pre-market high/low.
- Identify the overall trend and draw Volume Profiles for the previous day.

### Execution Rules
- **No Revenge Trading:** If a stop is hit, step away for 10 minutes.
- **No Overtrading:** Max 3 trades a day. Once the daily limit is hit (win or loss), close the charts.
- **Breakeven Rule:** Move stop loss to breakeven once price reaches 1R profit.

### End of Day Routine
- Screenshot every taken trade (both winners and losers).
- Log the trades in a journal (Entry, Exit, R:R, Emotions, Setup Type).
- Grade the day based on *rule execution*, not PnL.
